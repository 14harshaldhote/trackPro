Frequently Asked Questions

How do I delete a task? v

Open the task menu (three dots) and select Delete, or swipe left on mobile devices.

Can I export my data? v

Yes, go to Settings > Account > Export Data to download a JSON file.

How is streak calculated? v

Streaks increase every day you complete at least one task.
