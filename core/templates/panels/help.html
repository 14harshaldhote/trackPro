Getting Started

How do I create my first tracker?

Click the New Tracker button in the sidebar or press N. Give your tracker a name, choose a type, and add your tasks.

What's the difference between tracker types?
  • Daily - Tasks reset each day
  • Weekly - Tasks reset each week
  • Custom - Set your own schedule
How do I mark tasks as complete?

Click the checkbox next to any task, or select it and press Space. You can also right-click for more options.

Using Trackers

Can I reorder tasks?

Yes! Drag tasks using the handle on the left side. Your order is automatically saved.

How do I set goals?

Go to the Goals panel from the sidebar. Click "New Goal" and choose your target type: task count, streak days, or completion rate.

What do the colors on the heatmap mean?

Darker colors = more tasks completed that day. Hover over any cell to see the exact count.
